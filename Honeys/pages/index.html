Basking in the Welsh sun, Honeys’s cafe and bakery is nestled in the Caergwrle village, overlooking the picturesque River Alyn. With ample parking and disabled access the whole family (including the family dog) can enjoy this tranquil oasis away from the busy outside world.

Don’t forget to sample some of our award winning cream cheese buns and white crusty loaves among other delights in our bakery.
